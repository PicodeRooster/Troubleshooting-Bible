title: Role-Based Access Control  (RBAC)
type: dictionary
tags:
  - permissions
  - security
sources:

---
RBCA assigns access based on roles within the organization. These roles are predefined, consistent and easily assigned. RBCA improves efficiency and security, ensures users receive the correct permissions automatically according to their roles. Also makes transitions like promotions or departures, more manageable. Supports compliance frameworks by documenting who has access to what and why. Periodic audition is required to keep this model effective so that users do not accumulate roles they don't need.

**Site plans** are network documents that map the physical layout of an environment. They cover equipment locations (rack units, wall mounts, IDF/MDF rooms), cable runs and conduit paths, power and cooling layout, and wireless access point coverage zones.

**Best practice:** Maintain a master copy and a working version. Update diagrams after every hardware or topology change.

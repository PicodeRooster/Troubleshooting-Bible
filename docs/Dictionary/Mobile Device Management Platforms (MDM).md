Mobile Device Management (MDM) platforms are crucial programs containing tools that allow IT administrators to enforce policies, push updates and wipe data on a wide fleet of registered enterprise machines. They offer centralized security maintenance and monitoring of company-owned devices, allowing teams to mitigate risks and achieve compliance requirements through one remote framework. It is especially critical to implement in BYOD environments.

	**Examples:**
		*Intune*, *Jamf*, **IBM MaaS360

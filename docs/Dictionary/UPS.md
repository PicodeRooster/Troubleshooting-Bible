Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) acts as a safety buffer during power outages, providing enough battery runtime to allow systems to shut down gracefully, or remain online briefly enough until a generator kicks in. Higher-end models offer real-time monitoring, remote management, and power conditioning.

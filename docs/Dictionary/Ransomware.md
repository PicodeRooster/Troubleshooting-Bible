Encrypts user data and demands a ransom for the decryption key. Common in phishing attacks or exploited vulnerabilities, ransomware can paralyze entire companies with some variants even threatening to leak stolen data. Prevention, segmentation, and offline backups are a technicians strongest defenses against this kind of attack.
